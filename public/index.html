Real-time Baby Monitor

This real-time baby monitor uses your device's camera and microphone to capture video and audio data. It analyzes the data in real time to monitor the baby's movements and sounds. The information is displayed in various charts, which help you understand the baby's state in detail. In case of any abnormal activity, the system will alert you.

Camera and detected Keypoints/Skelton

Features

The charts below illustrate the analysis of different features from the video and audio data.

Total Movement

Shows the total movement detected in the video frame.

Head Movement

Shows the movement of the head detected in the video frame.

Hand and Leg Movement

Shows the movement of the hands and legs detected in the video frame.

Body Length

Shows the calculated body length in the video frame.

Body Orientation

Shows the calculated body orientation in the video frame.

Nose Detected

Indicates whether the nose was detected in the video frame.

Chest and Abdomen Movement

Shows the movement of the chest and abdomen detected in the video frame.

Power Sum 2kHz to 4kHz

Shows the power sum of frequencies between 2kHz and 4kHz in the audio data.
